Doctors' Room

This is a hospital with horrible monsters roaming around and you need to escape from it.

You can find some hints in room 413, go through the dark corridor and get to doctors' room to find the key to the front gate. Then you need to go back to the corridor and get out from the front door.

Doctors' Room

Hints

Here, you can find the key to get out.

However, you still need to be careful. There are some sleeping monsters, and you should avoid touching them
